Mission

Develop robust and resilient AI models to detect, withstand, and mitigate emerging AI-driven cyber threats. The TrustSec AI Lab advances research at the intersection of AI and cybersecurity, building AI-driven solutions that enhance the security, robustness, and trustworthiness of interconnected digital systems.

Adversarial AI: attacks, robustness analysis, and certified defenses.
